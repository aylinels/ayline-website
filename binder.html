Dealing with disaster,
lessons from the 2020 pandemic

/2020

This publication includes three articles found from various newspapers reflecting on the 2020 pandemic that had to be deemed informational for a future pandemic. I chose the color dark orange and the rectangle as a graphical repetition to link all three articles. I created this very strict layout, playing only with rectangle elements for each page to create variety, as well as a personal challenge to master white spaces and text wrap.
