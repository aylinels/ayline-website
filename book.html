Wikipedia Book: Adam & Eve

/2019

Using the content from a single Wikipedia entry, I had to create a multi-page book that mixes text and image; incorporates grid systems and typographic hierarchy; thoughtful typographic selections; and considers rhythm, contrast, and visual interest. I had to develop a complex grid structure and design system through which I had to use across every page of my book while considering how to translate content from one medium (the web) to another (a book) as well as how to translate the content into visual spreads.

I chose the Wikipedia page of Adam & Eve, as I am not only fond of Renaissance paintings but also this myth that has marked our humanity. When creating this book, I decided to choose a minimalistic and modern approach to the subject to contrast with the heavily-detailed paintings, but also as a way to bring lightness and accessibility to this serious matter. The colors red and pink, as well as the circle, is a recurrent theme throughout, reflecting respectively the apple and their flushed skin.
