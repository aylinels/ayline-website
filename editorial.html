Designer Profile: Ed Benguiat

/2019

This magazine spread was made in honor of the famous New York typographer: Edward Benguiat. I had to create a two spread magazine that needed to have a clear grid system, a strong layout, and a mix of images and text to reflect his years of typography. I decided to immerse the reader into the colorful, bold, and funky world of Benguiat, which I hope does him justice.
