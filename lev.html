Web Design: Les étoiles vagabondes

/2020

This website is dedicated to a rap album that particularly touched me called "Les étoiles vagabondes" ("The Wandering Stars") by the French rapper Nekfeu. Completely infatuated, I decided to create a website that would immerse the audience into the same world that the album had projected me in. Each image was delicately chosen in order to reflect the beat, message, or mood that I felt. As the album used an ongoing metaphor of the universe, I decided to illustrate it through vibrant abstract images, representing the world I evaded in- a world devoid of dimensions and clear shapes, but also heavily colored. The typography is very minimal, leaving space for the music and images to compose this wandering universe.
