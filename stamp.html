City Stamp: Castellon

/2019

Many mid-century postage stamps are gems in graphic design history. They’re simple but highly functional, often composed of basic geometric shapes like rectangles, circles, and triangles that evoke various national or local symbols in a minimal manner. The city assigned was Castellón de la Plana in Spain representing here with basic geometry las Islas Columbretes, which are a group of uninhabited islands on the eastern shore of the city.
